Double Irrigate

CHANGEQ

2021-07-20 00:00:00

所获奖项 红点概念设计奖

本设计是一款结合了地插式和手持式两种使用方式的灌溉喷水器，它同时满足了自动360°灌溉和人工灌溉两种需求。传统的喷水器灌溉方式单一，因而用户需要购置多个不同功能的喷水产品来弥补单一产品的短板。而这款新的喷水器能让用户通过简单的旋转操作快速实现两种使用方式的切换，极大减少了灌溉的操作成本，减少了产品数量，并且提高了灌溉的效率。

“Double irrugate” is an irrigation sprinkler that combines two usage modes: ground-inset and hand-held. It meets both the needs of automatic 360° irrigation and manual irrigation. Since the irrigation method of traditional sprinkler is single, users need to purchase multiple sprinkler products with different functions to make up for the shortcomings of one product. The new sprinklers enable users to switch between the two modes quickly through simple rotation operations, which not only greatly reduce the operating cost of irrigation and the number of products required, but also improve the efficiency of irrigation.
