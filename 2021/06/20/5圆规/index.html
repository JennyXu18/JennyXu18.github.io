protractor compasseses

CHANGEQ

2021-06-20 00:00:00

所获奖项 IDEA Finalist & K-design

本设计是一个具有量角器功能的圆规，它简化了交替使用圆规和量角器的画流程。借助圆规支腿上的刻度，用户能够直接用圆规画出预设角度的弧线，并准确测量出任意弧线的角度，大大提高了画图的效率和精度，减少了工具数量，更加便携。

This design is a pair of compasses with protractor function. It simplifies the drawing process of using compass and protractor alternately. With the scale on the compasses legs, users can directly draw arcs with preset angles and accurately measure the angle of any arc, which greatly improves the efficiency and accuracy of drawing, reduces the number of tools, and is more portable.
